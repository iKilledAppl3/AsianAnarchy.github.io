Compatible with iOS 7.0 - 8.1

Colorize your badges! ColorBadges will modify badges based on the app icon colors. To increase contrast. ColorBadges will also add an outline around the badge.

ColorBadges will colorize folder badges on the highest numbered badges inside the folder (or the first badges if none of them are numbered).

ColorBadges works great with most themes and is compatible with Badge Customizer - you can use it to change the position and alpha level of the badges if you so please.

ColorBadges supports iOS 7/8 devices, including your iPhones, iPads, iPod touches, and arm64 devices.
